"Sunshine Pet World Portal" is your project title and you're focusing on the Customer functionality aspect, here are some points you could consider:

**Registration and Login:**
• Provide a seamless registration process for new customers.
• Allow customers to log in securely with username/email and password.

**Profile Management:**
• Enable customers to update their personal information (name, address, contact details).
• Allow them to manage their preferences (communication settings, pet interests)

**Pet Details:**
• Provide detailed information about each pet (breed, age, health status).

• Include a support section where customers can submit queries or issues.
• Implement a ticketing system for tracking customer inquiries and resolutions.

• Enable customers to leave reviews and ratings for products or services.
• Gather feedback through surveys to improve customer experience.
These functionalities aim to enhance the overall customer experience within the Sunshine Pet World Portal, making it user-friendly and engaging for pet owners and enthusiasts.
