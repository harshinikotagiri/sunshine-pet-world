HANDLER

The Handler Module is a key component of the Sunshine Pet World platform. It is designed to streamline the management and care of pets within the system.

*Pet Management:*
• Allows handlers to add, update, and remove pet profiles, including detailed information such as species, breed, age, health records, and special care instructions.

*Task Scheduling:*
• Enables handlers to schedule and track tasks related to pet care, such as feeding, grooming, medical check-ups, and exercise routines.
• It includes automated reminders and notifications to ensure timely completion of tasks.

*Health Monitoring:*
• Provides tools for recording and monitoring the health status of pets.
• Handlers can log symptoms, medications, and vet visits, ensuring a comprehensive health history for each pet.

*Communication Tools:*
• Facilitates communication between handlers, pet owners, and veterinary professionals.
• It includes messaging features, appointment scheduling, and the ability to share updates and reports.

*Reports and Analytics:*
• Generates detailed reports and analytics on various aspects of pet care, helping handlers to make informed decisions and improve the quality of care.

*Integration with Other Modules:*
• Seamlessly integrates with other modules of the Sunshine Pet World platform, such as the owner module,
vet module, and inventory management, ensuring a cohesive and efficient workflow.
This module is designed to enhance the efficiency of pet care, ensuring that every pet receives the best possible attention and care from their handlers.
