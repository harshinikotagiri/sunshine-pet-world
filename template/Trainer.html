TRAINER

The Trainer Module in the Sunshine Pet World platform is designed to facilitate professional training for pets,
ensuring they receive proper education and behavior management.

*Training Program Development:*
• Allows trainers to create and customize training programs tailored to the specific needs of different pets.
• Programs can include obedience training, agility training, behavior modification, and more.

*Session Scheduling:*
• Enables trainers to schedule training sessions with pets, including one-on-one sessions and group classes.
• It provides calendar integration and automated reminders to ensure trainers and pet owners are informed of upcoming sessions.

*Training Resources:*
• Offers access to a library of training materials, including videos, articles, and guides.
• Trainers can share these resources with pet owners to reinforce training at home.

*Behavioral Assessments:*
• Includes tools for conducting behavioral assessments to identify areas of improvement and tailor training strategies accordingly.
• This helps in addressing specific behavioral issues and ensuring effective training.

*Owner Involvement:*
• Facilitates communication and collaboration between trainers and pet owners.
• It includes features for sharing progress updates, training tips, and feedback,
encouraging active participation from pet owners. The Trainer Module aims to deliver comprehensive and effective training solutions,
enhancing the skills and behavior of pets and fostering a positive relationship between pets and their owners.
