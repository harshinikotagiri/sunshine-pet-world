HELPER

The Helper Module in the Sunshine Pet World platform is designed to support and enhance the daily operations of the pet care environment.

*Assistance Requests:*
• Allows pet owners and handlers to request assistance for various tasks, such as feeding, walking, grooming, or medical attention.
• Requests are tracked and managed to ensure timely responses.

*Task Assignment:*
• Automatically assigns tasks to available helpers based on their skills, availability, and proximity.
• This ensures efficient use of resources and timely completion of tasks.

*Volunteer Coordination:*
• Manages the scheduling and coordination of volunteers who assist with pet care.
• It includes tools for volunteer registration, shift management, and task allocation.

*Resource Management:*
• Tracks and manages resources such as pet supplies, equipment, and medications.
• Ensures that helpers have access to the necessary resources to complete their tasks effectively.

*Training and Certification:*
• Provides training materials and certification programs for helpers, ensuring they are well-prepared and qualified to perform their duties.
• This includes online courses, manuals, and assessments.

*Feedback and Evaluation:*
• Collects feedback from pet owners and handlers on the performance of helpers.
• This feedback is used to evaluate and improve the quality of assistance provided.
The Helper Module aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of pet care operations,
providing robust support to handlers and ensuring that pets receive consistent and high-quality care.
